# coronavirus-news-web-crawler

A script for extracting news articles and headlines on the topic - "Coronavirus".

The news articles are collected from the following sources:
- The New York Times
- USA TODAY
- Time
- Yahoo! News
- Science News
- The New Yorker
- Los Angeles Times
- New Scientist
- Scientific American
- Science News for Students
- The Week
- DOGO News
- The Onion
- National Review
- Live Science

Running Instruction:

	$ pipenv shell
	$ python webCrawler.py

Python Version:

	Python 3.8.5
